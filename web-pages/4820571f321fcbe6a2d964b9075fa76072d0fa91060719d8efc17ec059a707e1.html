About Us

Andrew Outreach Orphanage was established in 2016 by our founder, KIiza Abel, in the Bugiri district of Uganda. Our mission is to provide care, education, and support to orphaned and vulnerable children in our community.

We are located at P.O. Box 78 (U) and can be reached at +256709401422.

Our Mission

To provide a safe and nurturing environment for orphaned children, promoting their physical, emotional, and spiritual development through education, healthcare, and Christian values.
