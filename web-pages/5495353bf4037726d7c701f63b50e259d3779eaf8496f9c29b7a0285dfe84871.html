About Us

Established in 2016 by CEO KIiza Abel, Andrew Outreach Orphanage is dedicated to providing care, education, and support to children in need in the Bugiri district of Uganda.

Our Mission

To provide a loving and supportive environment for orphaned and vulnerable children, helping them to grow spiritually, academically, and socially.
