Our Lady of Fatima

Our Lady of Fatima is a title for the Virgin Mary in her appearance to three shepherd children in Fátima, Portugal, in 1917.

The apparitions are believed to have occurred on May 13, July 13, and August 13, 1917, with a final apparition on October 13, 1917.

According to tradition, the apparitions were witnessed by Lucia dos Santos and her cousins, Jacinta and Francisco Marto.

During the apparitions, the Virgin Mary reportedly encouraged prayer, repentance, and devotion to her Immaculate Heart.
